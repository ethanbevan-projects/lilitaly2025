    About Venue Where Contact

    Welcome to
    Lil Italy

    Cozy family-run Italian bistro & café in Södermalm, Stockholm. Authentic food, coffee, cocktails, lunch & dinner. Warm, casual, modern Italian food.

    With family roots in Italy and over a decade in hospitality, we blend tradition with a modern touch. From morning espresso to late-night cocktails, our bistro welcomes everyone. Book online or walk in anytime.

    YOUR NEW FAVORITE
    CAFE, BISTRO, RESTAURANT AND BAR.

    Rooted in 13 years of experience, Lil Italy is a true family business with deep Italian roots. Every detail—from the espresso machine to imported ingredients—is authentically Italian. We’re not just a café, bistro, restaurant, and bar, but a welcoming, multi-concept space designed for everyone, any time of day.

    All-day Italian hub: café mornings, bistro lunches, restaurant dinners, bar nights— one venue, four experiences, always authentic.
