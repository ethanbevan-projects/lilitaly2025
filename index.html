Welcome to Lil Italy

Cozy family-run Italian bistro & café in Södermalm, Stockholm. Authentic food, coffee, cocktails, lunch & dinner. Warm, casual, modern Italian food.

With family roots in Italy and over a decade in hospitality, we blend tradition with a modern touch. From morning espresso to late-night cocktails, our bistro welcomes everyone. Book online or walk in anytime.

Our Story

We are a family-run Italian restaurant serving handmade pasta, wood-fired pizza, and fine wines from Italy.

Our Food Truck

Catch us around the city! Bringing Italian street food to festivals and private events.

Farms & Friends

We partner with local farms for organic produce and sustainable meats. Community and freshness first.
